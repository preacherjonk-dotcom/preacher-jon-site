Mission: Jesus at the Center—Truth & Grace

Christ found me in the storm, not after it. He met me in the hospital room, the courtroom, the car that wouldn’t start—places where hope usually goes to die. Out of those ashes He called me to preach the gospel with open Bible, open heart, and open hands.

“Faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God.” — Romans 10:17 (KJV)

This ministry exists to do three simple things with relentless love: put Scripture into people’s hands, bring their needs to the Father in prayer, and show up with the presence of Christ—without pressure, with gentleness and respect. We keep the timeline short and the doors wide: if someone asks for a Bible, we send one. If someone asks for prayer, we pray and follow up as desired. If someone asks for hope, we point to Jesus.

I’ve watched the Lord redeem suffering that seemed beyond repair. I’ve seen Him turn scars into testimony, sin into repentance, and despair into unexplainable joy. Wherever He sends me—rideshare cars, living rooms, pulpits, sidewalks—my message is the same: there is a Savior who knows your name, who bled for your sin, who rose for your life, and who walks with you through the valley you’re in right now.

“The LORD is nigh unto them that are of a broken heart.” — Psalm 34:18 (KJV)

Would you join me? Some will go with time—driving, listening, praying. Some will go with gifts—putting Bibles and resources in the mail. All of us can go with love, because love is the badge of the disciples of Jesus.
